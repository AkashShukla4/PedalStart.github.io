FAQ's

When does the program start?

The schedule of the weekly discussions will be shared with the selected startups one week prior.

Will it be live or pre- recorded?

The sessions are going to be live, but a recorded video will be provided to the startups, post session for easy access whenever required.

Which stage startups are ideal to join the cohort?

The cohort is open to all kinds of startups, but ideally the early stage startups would be benefitted the most by it.

What is the evaluation process for the selected startups?

Based on the data shared by you, the team at pedalstart will go through your details and evaluate your fitment in the cohort

What is the program fee?

Zero-to-One is a paid program priced at INR 20,000/-

Is there any selection criteria to join the cohort?

Yes, it’s a selection based cohort where best 50 startups will be chosen on basis of their idea, stage and founder profile.

What is the timeline of the program?

Zero-to-One is a 6 week program

What is equity gullak?

Equity gullak is a equity exchange program where top 5 startups will get a chance to exchange their 1% equity with 1% of another entity at a higher valuation.

I don’t have a running startup but just an idea, should I enrol?

This program is only for startup founders who are live with their idea

Will I need to travel?

Zero-to-One is completely a 6 week virtual program.

What PedalStart community offers to startup founders?

PedalStart is a selection based community for startup founders to help them in executing their startup journey by assigning a dedicated mate, connect with successful founders and investment partners.

Still have doubts?

Still have doubts, hit us up at zerotoone@pedalstart.com
